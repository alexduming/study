## Introduction

Welcome to **StudyHacks** (`studyhacks.ai`), an AI-powered learning platform that helps students transform PDFs, videos, lectures, and audio into smart notes, flashcards, quizzes, and study guides.  
Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains **what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it** when you use StudyHacks.

By accessing or using StudyHacks, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

## Information We Collect and How We Use It

To provide and improve our services, we may collect the following categories of information:

1. **Account Information**
   - **What We Collect**: Your name, email address, password (stored in hashed form), and any profile information you choose to provide.
   - **Why We Collect It**: To create and manage your StudyHacks account, authenticate logins, and provide customer support.

2. **Study Content and Uploaded Materials**
   - **What We Collect**: Documents, PDFs, lecture notes, videos, audio recordings, and other study materials you upload or connect to StudyHacks.
   - **Why We Collect It**: To generate AI-powered notes, flashcards, quizzes, podcasts, and other study tools as described on our landing page.
   - **Important Note**: We do not claim ownership of your original study materials; they remain yours. We only process them to provide the StudyHacks features you request.

3. **Usage Details**
   - **What We Collect**: Information about how you use StudyHacks, such as:
     - Features you access (e.g., AI note taker, flashcards, quizzes, podcasts)
     - Actions you perform (e.g., uploads, quiz sessions, generated content)
     - Session duration and interaction patterns
   - **Why We Collect It**: To analyze feature usage, improve the learning experience, personalize recommendations, and ensure platform reliability and security.

4. **Device and Technical Information**
   - **What We Collect**: IP address, device type, operating system, browser type, language settings, and approximate location (based on IP).
   - **Why We Collect It**: To optimize StudyHacks for different devices, troubleshoot issues, prevent abuse, and ensure compatibility and security.

5. **Cookies and Similar Technologies**
   - **What We Collect**: Small data files stored on your device (cookies, local storage, or similar technologies).
   - **Why We Collect It**:
     - To keep you signed in and maintain your session
     - To remember your language, theme, and study preferences
     - To measure traffic and usage patterns so we can improve StudyHacks
   - **Your Choices**: You can manage cookies through your browser settings, but disabling certain cookies may affect core StudyHacks functionality.

6. **Payment and Billing Information**
   - **What We Collect**: When you subscribe to a paid plan, our payment providers (such as Stripe or other processors configured in the app) may collect:
     - Payment method details (e.g., credit card number, billing address)
     - Transaction identifiers, subscription status, and billing history
   - **Why We Collect It**: To process payments, manage subscriptions, prevent fraud, and comply with financial and legal obligations.
   - **Note**: StudyHacks itself does not store full payment card numbers. This information is handled by secure third‑party payment processors.

7. **Support and Communication Data**
   - **What We Collect**: Messages you send to our support team, feedback, survey responses, and email communication preferences.
   - **Why We Collect It**: To respond to your requests, improve our services, and send you important updates about StudyHacks.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for purposes including:

- **Providing and maintaining the service**
  - Operating core features such as AI note generation, flashcards, quizzes, podcasts, and progress tracking
- **Personalizing your learning experience**
  - Recommending study tools, adjusting difficulty levels, and adapting to your learning style as described on the landing page
- **Improving StudyHacks**
  - Analyzing aggregated usage data to improve features, performance, and user experience
- **Security and abuse prevention**
  - Detecting suspicious activity, preventing misuse, protecting accounts, and enforcing our Terms of Service
- **Legal and compliance**
  - Fulfilling our legal obligations, such as responding to lawful requests and maintaining appropriate records

We process your data on the basis of:

- Your consent (e.g., optional analytics, marketing emails)
- Contractual necessity (to provide the StudyHacks service you sign up for)
- Legitimate interests (e.g., improving features, preventing abuse), where these are not overridden by your privacy interests

## Data Storage, Retention, and Security

We store your information on secure servers and apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect it, including:

- Encryption in transit (HTTPS) and, where appropriate, at rest
- Access controls to limit who can access personal and study data
- Regular monitoring and maintenance of our infrastructure

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we work hard to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We retain your data for as long as necessary to:

- Provide the StudyHacks service
- Comply with legal obligations
- Resolve disputes and enforce our agreements

You can request deletion of your account and associated personal data as described in the “Your Rights and Choices” section below.

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do **not** sell your personal information. We only share it in the following situations:

1. **Service Providers and Processors**
   - We work with trusted third-party providers (e.g., hosting, analytics, payment processing, email delivery) that process data on our behalf under data protection agreements.

2. **Legal and Safety**
   - We may disclose information if we believe it is reasonably necessary to:
     - Comply with applicable laws, legal processes, or lawful government requests
     - Enforce our Terms of Service
     - Protect the rights, property, or safety of StudyHacks, our users, or others

3. **Business Transfers**
   - If StudyHacks is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you if such a change in ownership or control occurs and if it affects your personal data.

4. **With Your Consent**
   - We may share information with third parties when you explicitly authorize us to do so (for example, integrations you enable).

## Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

- **Access**: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
- **Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal or contractual obligations
- **Restriction**: Request that we limit how we process your data in certain circumstances
- **Portability**: Request a structured, machine‑readable copy of your data where technically feasible
- **Objection**: Object to certain types of processing (e.g., direct marketing)

You can exercise some of these rights directly in your account settings (where available) or by contacting us at [support@studyhacks.ai](mailto:support@studyhacks.ai).

If you receive marketing emails from us, you can opt out at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in those emails.

## International Data Transfers
